# UCM Instagram Marketing Plan

## Commercial Role

Use Instagram to make UCM's service quality visible, build local familiarity and turn job evidence into quote enquiries.

## Priority Audiences

- Time-poor London professionals.
- Landlords and tenants preparing handovers.
- Property managers and block managers.
- Office and hospitality buyers researching suppliers.
- New-build residents in priority growth zones.

## Offers

- Office and commercial cleaning.
- End-of-tenancy and Handover Complete.
- Communal-area and property-manager care plans.
- Specialist and technical cleaning.
- Cleaning plus responsive maintenance.

## Monthly Baseline

- Fifteen adapted feed assets.
- Four to six Reels within that total.
- Ten Stories.
- At least two proof-led assets and one direct quote asset.

## Formats

- Reels: 9:16, 1080x1920, 15-45 seconds, hard captions and a visible opening hook.
- Carousels: buyer checklists, process, mistakes and service comparisons.
- Static: approved proof, offers, reviews and local messages.
- Stories: polls, questions, job progress, checklist links and quote reminders.

## Content Series

- Proof, Not Promises.
- London Building Reset.
- Handover Detail Check.
- Carlos on Site.
- What Your Cleaner Missed.
- One Supplier, Fewer Headaches.

## Conversion

- Primary CTA: visit the quote page.
- Secondary CTA: send `QUOTE` by direct message.
- Manual qualification captures service, postcode, timing, property type and contact permission.
- Tracking: `utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=organic_social`.

## Approval

Before/after media, visible customer details, addresses, people, logos and testimonials require recorded permission. Reels using AI or sustainability claims must pass the claims register.

## KPIs

- Qualified direct messages.
- Profile visits and bio-link clicks.
- Quote starts and completions.
- Saves, shares and meaningful comments.
- Three-second hold and video completion.
- Qualified leads, contracts and revenue assisted by Instagram.

## First 90 Days

### Days 1-30

- Correct profile, bio, service area, contact actions and quote link.
- Publish the first proof, office, handover and founder assets.
- Establish a reusable Story and Reel design system.

### Days 31-60

- Test priority-zone Reels and property-manager education.
- Add the first approved testimonial or case study.
- Compare hooks by retention and quote traffic.

### Days 61-90

- Retarget engaged visitors only after tracking and landing pages are ready.
- Double down on service/zone combinations producing qualified enquiries.
- Retire formats that create reach without business relevance.
