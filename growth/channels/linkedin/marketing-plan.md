# UCM LinkedIn Marketing Plan

## Commercial Role

Use LinkedIn to create B2B trust through two connected voices:

- Carlos Cuevas: founder experience, judgement and relationships.
- UCM company page: services, proof, people, case studies and company news.

Carlos's canonical profile is https://www.linkedin.com/in/carlos-cuevas-ba1bbb35.

## Audience

- Facilities and workplace managers.
- Property, block and estate managers.
- Operations directors.
- Developers and new-build operators.
- Letting agents and landlords.
- Buyers responsible for technical or high-risk spaces.

## Founder Position

Recommended headline:

> Founder, UCM Services | Commercial Cleaning, Specialist Technical Cleaning and Responsive Property Care across London

Carlos writes as an experienced operator, not as a generic motivational creator.

## Cadence And Formats

- Eight Carlos posts.
- Four UCM company-page posts.
- Two native videos adapted from the shared content supply.
- Three focused commenting sessions per week.
- Up to ten relevant manual connection requests per week.

No automated connection or direct-message spam.

## Founder Series

- 30 Years, 30 Lessons.
- Carlos on Site.
- What Commercial Buyers Should Ask.
- Technical Cleaning Then and Now.
- One Supplier, Fewer Headaches.
- Building Standards That Reduce Complaints.

## Company Content

- Approved case studies and client outcomes.
- Care plans and service explainers.
- Team, process, training and quality checks.
- Recruitment and company updates.
- Building Health Report insights.

## CTA And Tracking

- Primary CTA: book a site visit or request a commercial quote.
- Add the best landing page to Carlos's Featured section.
- Tracking: `utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=organic_social&utm_campaign=founder_authority` for Carlos and a company-specific campaign value for UCM posts.

Use profile-specific UTM codes and record founder-assisted enquiries in the CRM. Attribute a lead to LinkedIn only when the buyer reports the source, uses the tracked destination or has a recorded content touchpoint.

## Approval

Carlos approves founder posts and video. UCM approves customer, site, client-name and service claims. The growth lead confirms the content manifest, claims status, destination CTA and tracking before publication.

## KPIs

- Relevant buyer connections.
- Target-buyer comments and direct conversations.
- Profile and company-page visits.
- Site visits and commercial quote requests.
- Qualified pipeline and won contract value.

- Publish the first approved company proof post.

### Days 61-90
