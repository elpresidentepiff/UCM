# UCM Facebook Marketing Plan

## Commercial Role

Use Facebook for local trust, service proof, customer reviews, Meta retargeting and direct response through message, call and quote actions.

Canonical UCM page: https://www.facebook.com/ucmservicesuk

## Audience

- London households and professionals.
- Landlords and tenants.
- Local property managers and small businesses.
- Existing customers who may review, refer or buy another service.

## Cadence And Formats

- Twelve to fifteen adapted feed posts.
- Four Reels within the shared video supply.
- Ten Stories.
- Consistent review, service and local proof activity.

## Content Series

- Local Job Proof.
- Handover Complete.
- UCM Team and Process.
- Cleaning Plus Maintenance.
- Customer Question of the Week.
- London Growth-Zone Offers.

## Page Standard

- Accurate phone, email, service area, hours and quote URL.
- Clear service list and approved photographs.
- Quote-focused page CTA.
- Messenger response expectations and human handoff.
- Professional, specific responses to genuine reviews.

## CTA And Tracking

- Organic CTA: quote page, message or call.
- Tracking: `utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=organic_social`.
- Paid campaigns use dedicated landing pages or Meta lead forms with qualification fields.
- Retarget only after consent, conversion tracking and lead-quality reporting are ready.

Use post, campaign and offer-specific UTM codes plus a dedicated WhatsApp source field. Record calls and messages as qualified only after service, postcode, urgency and buyer fit are confirmed.

## Approval

Organic and paid assets require claims review, consent status, human approval and a valid destination. Retargeting audiences and message data must follow the documented privacy basis and platform permissions.

## Paid Media Rules

- Start with office/commercial, property-manager and handover offers.
- Define audience, offer, creative, CTA, conversion event and stop-loss before launch.
- Do not scale spend when the landing page, tracking or follow-up process is weak.
- Report cost per qualified lead, not only cost per form.

## KPIs

- Calls, messages and quote submissions.
- Qualified leads and cost per qualified lead.
- Video completion and landing-page clicks.
- Genuine review volume and response time.
- Won revenue and repeat work influenced by Facebook.

## 90-Day Execution

### Days 1-30

- Correct page information and CTA.
- Publish proof, handover, office and founder content.
- Define Messenger qualification and response ownership.

### Days 31-60

- Test local growth-zone creative organically.
- Activate genuine review requests after successful jobs.
- Prepare retargeting audiences and conversion checks.

### Days 61-90

- Launch a controlled campaign only if tracking and follow-up pass readiness checks.
- Reallocate budget using lead quality and won-pipeline evidence.
