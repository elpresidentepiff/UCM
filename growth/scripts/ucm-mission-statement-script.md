# UCM Mission Statement Script

## Format

- Speaker: Carlos and selected UCM team members.
- Target duration: 60 to 75 seconds.
- Use: website, recruitment, proposals, social media and company presentations.

## Script

"At UCM, our mission is to make cleaning and property care dependable, accountable and easier to prove.

We exist to protect the places where people live and work, reduce the pressure on those responsible for buildings, and deliver a standard clients can see and trust.

We listen before we scope. We build the service around the property, the people using it and the outcome required. We combine professional cleaning, specialist capability and practical property care with clear responsibility and human support.

We use technology to remove administrative waste, improve communication and make service evidence more useful. Technology supports our people; it never replaces judgement, safety or accountability.

We pursue environmental improvement through practical action and honest measurement: correct product use, less waste, smarter journeys and better information. We do not make promises the evidence cannot support.

We respect every customer, every member of our team and every site entrusted to us. When something is wrong, we record it, take ownership and work to put it right.

Our ambition is to build long-term relationships, create better workplaces and homes, and set a stronger standard for modern cleaning and property care.

UCM: professional people, clear standards and work you can trust."

## Short Mission Statement

> UCM makes cleaning and property care dependable, accountable and easier to prove. We combine professional people, clear standards, practical technology and honest environmental improvement to protect the places where people live and work.
