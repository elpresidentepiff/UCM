# UCM Company Introduction Script

## Production Status

This script is approved as a working draft, not as a published factual record. Before filming, UCM must verify every statement about training, insurance, accreditations, products, waste handling, measurement and client history. Remove any line that cannot be evidenced.

## 90-Second Founder Script

**Opening: why we exist**

"A clean building is not just about appearance. It affects how people feel when they arrive, how safely a team can work, how confidently a facilities manager can report, and how much time is lost when small problems are ignored.

UCM exists to give people one reliable partner for cleaning and practical property care. Our roots go back to technical cleaning in 1994, where precision, security and attention to detail were not optional. That discipline still shapes how we work today."

**Why UCM is different**

"What makes us different is that we do not begin with a generic checklist. We begin with the building, the people using it, the required standard and the risks that have to be controlled.

We build a clear scope, assign responsibility, record what was completed and deal with issues before they become repeated complaints. Cleaning, specialist work and responsive maintenance can sit within one accountable service plan, with a real person responsible for the relationship."

**How UCM approaches compliance**

"Compliance is not a badge on a website. It is the work done before, during and after every service.

That means site and task risk assessments, COSHH assessments and current safety data sheets for relevant products, documented methods, appropriate training and protective equipment, secure-site instructions, incident reporting and controlled waste handling where those duties apply.

The exact controls depend on the site and the service. We agree them before work begins, keep the records available and review them when the work, products or risks change."

**How UCM supports carbon reduction**

"We also help clients reduce the cleaning-related part of their environmental footprint, but we will not make vague green promises.

The practical work comes first: correct chemical dosing instead of overuse, concentrated products where suitable, reusable systems where hygienically appropriate, route planning that can reduce unnecessary travel, efficient equipment, better stock control, and waste separation that matches the client's site arrangements.

Where reliable activity data is available, we can record measures such as mileage, fuel, electricity, product use and waste, then use recognised UK conversion factors to build a baseline and report progress. We only claim a reduction when the evidence shows one."

**Close**

"UCM is here to make property care clearer, safer and easier to prove. If you manage a home, workplace or portfolio of buildings, speak to us about the standard you need and the problems you want removed. We will build the right scope around your site, not force your site into a generic package."

## 30-Second Version

"UCM exists because cleaning should do more than make a building look presentable. It should protect people, reduce operational friction and give clients clear evidence of what was delivered.

Our approach combines tailored cleaning and property care with site-specific risk controls, COSHH documentation where relevant, trained people and accountable quality checks.

We also help reduce cleaning-related environmental impact through correct dosing, smarter routing, efficient equipment, better stock control and measurable reporting where reliable data exists.

No vague green promises. No generic checklist. A clear service built around your site."

## 15-Second Opening Reel

"A clean building should be safe, accountable and easy to prove. UCM combines tailored cleaning, practical compliance controls and measurable environmental improvements around the needs of each site. No generic checklist. No vague green promises."

## Claims That Must Not Be Used Yet

- "Fully compliant" or "100% compliant."
- "Carbon neutral," "net zero," "zero impact" or "planet safe."
- A percentage carbon, fuel, plastic, waste or cost reduction without a defined baseline and records.
- "Non-toxic," "chemical free" or "completely safe."
- Any accreditation, insurance level, client name or historic result that has not been verified for public use.
- A promise that every service includes every control listed above. Controls are site and task specific.

## Evidence Checklist Before Filming

- Founder history and dates approved.
- Current insurance schedule.
- Current accreditations and permitted logos.
- COSHH process, product inventory and safety data sheets reviewed.
- Risk assessment and method statement process reviewed.
- Training and PPE statements verified.
- Waste carrier and transfer responsibilities checked for the services shown.
- Products and dosing systems filmed are genuinely used by UCM.
- Site, customer, logo, employee and media permissions recorded.
- Environmental baseline method agreed before any quantified claim.
