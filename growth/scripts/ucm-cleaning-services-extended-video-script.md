# UCM Cleaning Services: Extended Video Script

## Format

- Speaker: Carlos or a professional British human voice.
- Audience: London office, property, landlord and facilities buyers.
- Target duration: 90 to 110 seconds.
- Purpose: replace the short synthetic-voice reel with a fuller UCM story.

## Script

"A building can look clean at first glance. But was the right work completed? Were the high-touch areas covered? Were problems recorded? Can the client prove the standard they paid for?

At UCM Cleaning Services, we believe cleaning should create confidence, not uncertainty.

Our roots trace back to technical cleaning in 1994, where precision, security and attention to detail were essential. Today, we bring that same discipline to offices, managed buildings, homes, handovers, specialist cleaning and practical property care across London.

We begin by understanding the site: who uses it, what standard is required, which areas matter most and what risks need to be controlled. Then we build a clear scope around the building instead of forcing every client into the same generic checklist.

Professional people complete the work. Clear methods guide the service. Quality checks, approved photographs and issue records can show what happened and what needs attention next.

For selected high-touch surfaces, UCM is developing before-and-after cleanliness verification. We are also developing a controlled HOCl disinfection service for suitable surfaces, using an approved product, its specified concentration and contact time, and human review. We will only make claims the final product evidence supports.

UCM can also connect cleaning with responsive maintenance and handover support. That means fewer suppliers, clearer responsibility and small property problems identified before they become expensive ones.

Technology helps us organise enquiries, prepare clearer scopes, reduce repetitive administration and report more consistently. It does not replace trained people, site controls or accountable customer service.

We are also working to reduce cleaning-related waste through correct dosing, responsible product use, smarter routing and measured improvement. We will show the evidence before claiming the result.

UCM Cleaning Services. Professional cleaning, practical property care and visible proof.

Cleaned. Checked. Documented.

Visit ucmservices.co.uk and request a service built around your property."

## Required Visuals

Use approved footage of Carlos, UCM staff, real cleaning work, a site walk, high-touch cleaning, a genuine checklist, before-and-after testing, maintenance observations and the final client record. Generated concept imagery must not be presented as a real UCM client site.
