# Carlos: Bringing UCM Into The Future

## Format

- Speaker: UCM Growth Agent or clearly disclosed digital presenter.
- Audience: Carlos Cuevas, UCM owner.
- Tone: confident, respectful, commercially ambitious.
- Target duration: 2.5 to 3 minutes.

## Script

"Hello Carlos.

You have already built the part that technology cannot manufacture: real experience, client trust and a company with roots going back to technical cleaning in 1994.

My role is not to replace you or pretend to be you. I am the UCM Growth Agent: a digital extension of the standards, knowledge and ambition behind your company. My job is to help turn what UCM knows into a system that can sell, operate and grow every day.

First, we make UCM easier to buy. The website will speak directly to homeowners, landlords, office managers and property professionals. CleanScope will guide each enquiry through postcode, service, urgency, property details and approved photographs or video. Your team receives a clearer brief and can respond with a faster, human-reviewed scope.

Second, we build commercial control. The UCM CRM will show every lead, survey, quote, contract, renewal and next action. Nothing valuable should disappear inside an inbox or depend on one person's memory. Carlos and the team will see where revenue is coming from, which quotes need attention and which clients are ready for renewal or a wider service.

Third, we prove delivery. The customer hub and Proof Passport will give authorised clients access to completed checklists, approved before-and-after photographs, issues, documents and sign-off. UCM will not simply say the work was completed. We will make the standard visible.

Fourth, we turn genuine work into growth. The Media Engine will help transform approved job footage into reels, case studies, founder videos and local campaigns. Carlos on Site and Thirty Years, Thirty Lessons will make your experience part of the brand. The London Demand Radar will focus campaigns on offices, managed buildings, regeneration areas, new developments and the locations producing qualified enquiries.

Fifth, we create stronger products. Essential, Managed and Intelligent Care Plans can turn one-off cleaning into recurring relationships. Handover Complete can combine cleaning, minor repairs and evidence for landlords and developers. Building Health Reports can turn observations into useful client action. The HOCl Verified Disinfection pilot can add controlled disinfection and before-and-after cleanliness checks once the exact product and evidence are approved.

AI works behind this system to reduce repetitive administration, organise information, support clearer scopes, prepare content and identify opportunities. People remain responsible for prices, safety, claims, client decisions and service quality. This is not automation for show. It is technology serving the company.

The result is a UCM that responds faster, loses fewer opportunities, proves more of its work, builds recurring revenue and learns which services, areas and campaigns create profit.

Carlos, the future of UCM is not becoming less human. It is making your experience easier to access, easier to trust and easier to scale.

You built the reputation. Now we build the system around it."

## Production Note

Do not create an undisclosed impersonation of Carlos. A digital presenter must be clearly identified as the UCM Growth Agent. Client data, prices, safety decisions and public claims remain human-controlled.
