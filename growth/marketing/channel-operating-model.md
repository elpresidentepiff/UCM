# UCM Channel Operating Model

## One System, Different Jobs

UCM does not need identical copy sprayed across every platform. One monthly source calendar creates platform-specific executions.

| Channel | Commercial job | Monthly baseline | Primary conversion |
| --- | --- | ---: | --- |
| Instagram | Visual proof and local discovery | 15 adapted feed assets, 4-6 Reels, 10 Stories | Quote link or qualified DM |
| YouTube | Search authority and video library | 4 Shorts, 1-2 longer videos | Service landing page |
| LinkedIn | Founder authority and B2B contracts | 8 founder posts, 4 company posts, 2 native videos | Site visit or commercial quote |
| Facebook | Local trust and Meta conversion | 12-15 adapted posts, 4 Reels, 10 Stories | Quote, message or call |

## Shared Monthly Workflow

1. Choose one primary service, one secondary service and two target buyer groups.
2. Select target zones using public demand signals and UCM outcome data.
3. Confirm the offer, CTA, landing page and tracking before production.
4. Collect only approved real assets.
5. Build one content manifest per asset.
6. Adapt hook, duration, caption and CTA for each platform.
7. Run claims, proof and consent checks.
8. Obtain human approval.
9. Publish or schedule.
10. Measure reach, retention, enquiries, lead quality and revenue.

## Content Allocation

The 15 core monthly assets should normally contain:

- Four educational assets.
- Three service offers.
- Two proof or case-study assets.
- Two team or process assets.
- Two evidence-safe sustainability assets.
- One direct quote asset.
- One seasonal or local asset.

Each asset must answer a buyer question or reduce a purchase risk.

## Tracking Convention

Use:

`utm_source={platform}&utm_medium=organic_social&utm_campaign={campaign_id}&utm_content={content_id}`

Paid campaigns replace `organic_social` with the appropriate paid medium. Platform post IDs and final URLs return to the content manifest.

## Approval Gates

No asset publishes until:

- Service and location claims are accurate.
- Customer and media permissions are recorded.
- Reviews and client names are authentic and approved.
- Environmental and savings wording has evidence status.
- CTA and destination are live.
- Tracking parameters are present.
- A named person approves the final version.

## Reporting

Report by platform, campaign, service, zone, buyer, format, hook and CTA. Separate reach from commercially useful outcomes. The final scorecard is qualified leads, quotes, contracts, recurring revenue and gross margin.
