## Short-Form Structure

1. Hook in the first two seconds.
2. Show a real problem or buyer risk.
3. Demonstrate the UCM process.
4. Show evidence where approved.
5. State one clear CTA.

## Long-Form Structure

1. Buyer problem.
2. Site or service context.
3. UCM method.
4. Evidence and limitations.
5. Practical advice.
6. Offer and next step.
