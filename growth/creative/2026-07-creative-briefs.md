# UCM July 2026 Creative Briefs

## Creative Direction

Design should feel clean, professional and service-led. Avoid gimmicks. Use UCM green, charcoal, white space, real site photos, real team/process images and simple commercial messaging.

## Required Assets

- UCM logo files.
- Brand colour references.
- Real job photos.
- Team/process photos.
- Before/after images.
- Customer review screenshots or text.
- Cleaning equipment/product photos.

## Feed Creative Briefs

### 1. Office Cleaning London

Format: static graphic or real office image.

Message: "Office Cleaning Services in London"

Visual: clean office, reception, desk area or branded graphic with simple service list.

CTA overlay: "Request a quote"

### 2. Commercial Cleaning Checklist

Format: carousel.

Slides:

1. Commercial cleaning checklist.
2. Reception and entrances.
3. Workstations and touchpoints.
4. Kitchens and washrooms.
5. Floors, waste and periodic deep cleaning.
6. Need a tailored plan? Contact UCM.

### 3. Proof Beats Promises

Format: before/after image or reel.

Visual: real job result.

Important: do not use fake before/after imagery.

### 4. Property Management Cleaning

Format: static or carousel.

Message: "Cleaner communal areas. Fewer avoidable complaints."

Visual: communal hallway, entrance, stairwell or managed property space.

### 5. Good Cleaning Starts Before The First Shift

Format: carousel.

Slides:

1. Good cleaning starts before the first shift.
2. Scope.
3. Schedule.
4. Products and equipment.
5. Quality checks.
6. Communication.

### 6. Sustainable Cleaning

Format: static graphic.

Message: "Sustainable cleaning needs specifics, not slogans."

Visual: product/process shot, not generic leaves unless there is real proof.

### 7. End-Of-Tenancy Cleaning

Format: reel or carousel.

Visual: kitchen, bathroom, floors, handover-ready room.

Message: "Deposit-ready details matter."

### 8. IT Equipment Cleaning

Format: static graphic or short reel.

Visual: keyboard, workstation, server room, equipment cleaning.

Message: "Specialist cleaning for technical spaces."

### 9. Cleaning Plus Maintenance

Format: split graphic.

Left: cleaning.

Right: maintenance.

Message: "One property-care partner. Fewer supplier headaches."

### 10. Review/Testimonial

Format: quote graphic.

Visual: UCM branded template plus approved customer quote.

Important: only use real approved review copy.
