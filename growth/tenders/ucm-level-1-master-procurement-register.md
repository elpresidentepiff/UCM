# UCM Level 1 Master Procurement Register

**Status:** Active control file

**Stage:** Level 1 - contracts around GBP 50,000 and smaller supplier opportunities

**Last updated:** 23 July 2026

**Commercial owner:** Carlos Cuevas

**Delivery support:** UCM growth and procurement team

## Purpose

This is the single Level 1 procurement control file for UCM.

It records:

- the basic information Carlos has been asked to provide;
- what has already been found from public UCM sources;
- what the delivery team can create after Carlos supplies the facts;
- registration actions for the first supplier portals;
- the next action, owner and evidence location for every item.

This register is intentionally smaller than the full supplier-evidence guide. It is designed to make UCM credible for proportionate small-contract and subcontract opportunities without pretending that major-framework readiness is required now.

## Security Rule

This GitHub repository is public. Do not upload:

- bank details;
- tax references;
- accounts;
- insurance certificates containing sensitive information;
- employee records;
- DBS information;
- client contracts;
- personal contact details that are not already public.

The actual evidence belongs in a private UCM-controlled document library. This file records the status and private filename only.

## Status Key

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `KNOWN - VERIFY` | Public information exists but Carlos must confirm it is current and correct |
| `PARTIAL` | Some information exists but is not enough for a tender response |
| `WAITING FOR CARLOS` | Carlos must provide the document, fact or decision |
| `CONDITIONAL` | Only required for a relevant contract or role |
| `READY TO BUILD` | The delivery team can create the document after receiving the required facts |
| `TEMPLATE CREATED` | A controlled working template exists but still requires verified facts and approval |
| `DRAFT - AWAITING INPUTS` | A populated draft exists but cannot be issued until Carlos verifies the marked fields |
| `COMPLETE` | Verified evidence is stored privately and its review date is recorded |

## Decision Rules

- Do not submit a tender until insurance, price and delivery capacity have been checked.
- Do not use a client name, logo, reference or photograph without permission.
- Do not claim a certificate, registration or policy is complete until the evidence is stored privately and reviewed.
- Do not pursue a contract that requires unsupported school, clinical, technical, disinfection or national-delivery capability.
- Do not price from a competitor's rate. Price from UCM's real labour, supervision, product, equipment, travel, risk and margin costs.
- Do not upload sensitive procurement evidence to this public repository.
