## Dependable Cleaning, Maintenance and Service Evidence

Universal Cleaning & Maintenance Services Ltd (`company number 12265169` - **Carlos to verify**) provides cleaning and related property-support services for organisations that need reliable delivery, clear communication and visible quality control.

UCM combines experienced people with practical digital tools. Technology supports faster enquiries, clearer scopes, scheduling, service records and reporting. It does not replace trained operatives, supervision or human accountability.
