# UCM Level 1 Procurement Working Templates

**Status:** Working templates - not approved evidence

**Audience:** UCM staff preparing proportionate cleaning and maintenance bids

These templates are the first documents produced by the UCM delivery team. They are designed for smaller commercial opportunities and subcontract work while Carlos supplies the verified company, insurance, finance, staffing, pricing and client evidence recorded in the [master procurement register](../ucm-level-1-master-procurement-register.md).

## What Is Included

| File | Purpose | Approval needed before use |
|---|---|---|
| `ucm-capability-statement-draft.md` | Buyer-facing summary of UCM's offer and delivery controls | Carlos confirms every bracketed fact and approves the final version |
| `ucm-rams-master-template.md` | Structure for site- and task-specific risk assessment and method statements | Competent person completes, reviews and signs it for the actual site |
| `ucm-mobilisation-checklist.md` | Controls the period from contract award to first-week review | Contract owner and operations lead approve each mobilisation |
| `ucm-quality-and-proof-passport-template.md` | Records completion, inspection, approved evidence, defects and sign-off | Supervisor verifies the record; client acknowledgement where agreed |
| `ucm-coshh-register-template.csv` | Index of products, SDS files, assessments, controls and review dates | Competent person completes an assessment for each actual use |
| `ucm-document-control-register.csv` | Index of controlled tender and operational documents | Named owner maintains versions, approvals and review dates |

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- These files are templates, not proof that UCM already complies with a buyer requirement.
- Replace every `[TO CONFIRM]` field with verified information or remove it.
- Keep certificates, accounts, tax records, employee data, DBS information, client contracts and signed site records in UCM's private document library.
- A Safety Data Sheet is not a COSHH assessment. UCM must assess the actual product, task, exposure and controls.
- RAMS must reflect the real site, work, people, products and equipment. A generic unsigned template must never be issued as approved site RAMS.
- Proof Passport photographs require appropriate permission and must not expose people, security systems, confidential material or personal data.
- Proof Passport records show that an agreed service was completed. They do not prove sterility, pathogen removal or a disinfectant performance claim.
- Client names, logos, testimonials and contract values require written approval before external use.
- HOCl, fogging, mould-treatment and disinfection claims require the exact product authorisation, current SDS, approved use, concentration, contact time and evidence. Unsupported claims are blocked.

## Working Process

1. Copy the relevant template into the private opportunity folder.
2. Give the document a unique ID, owner, version and review date.
3. Complete it using verified UCM and site information.
4. Record supporting files in the document-control register.
5. Obtain the required human review and approval.
6. Issue only the approved version.
7. Record changes, incidents and lessons for the next version.
