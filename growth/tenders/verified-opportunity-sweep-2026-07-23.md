# UCM Verified Opportunity Sweep

**Research date:** 23 July 2026
**Purpose:** Separate real open opportunities from portals, expired notices, awarded contracts and speculative target lists.

## Executive Verdict

UCM does not currently have dozens of live London cleaning tenders. It has:

1. Three continuously open admission routes worth investigating.
2. One time-limited cleaning tender that is technically open but probably a commercial no-bid.
3. Three material London/housing pipelines that require direct status checks.
4. Several valuable FM-prime and housing supplier routes that are not tenders and must not be reported as applications.

The right strategy is to qualify for realistic dynamic markets, build the evidence pack once, monitor London notices and use prime-contractor relationships to reach work that is already awarded.

- **External status:** Open admission until 23 February 2029.
- **Current estimated ceiling:** GBP 2 billion excluding VAT.
- **UCM fit:** Register after evidence review.
- **Phone:** 0345 410 2222.

This is an access route, not a contract award. Buyers filter suppliers by service, building type, location and annual contract value, then run competitions. The notice explicitly says no business is guaranteed.

## Metropolitan Police Route

The Metropolitan Police says:

- GBP 5,000 to GBP 50,000 opportunities are published on CompeteFor.
- Contracts above GBP 50,000 are advertised through Coupa, Find a Tender and Contracts Finder.
- Shortlisting may require a Standard Selection Questionnaire, financial and technical evidence and relevant examples.
- Award decisions consider value, technical criteria and social value.
- Awarded suppliers must accept the Supplier Code of Conduct and are expected to address modern slavery.

This is a supplier route, not evidence of a live cleaning tender. See the [official Met supplier guidance](https://www.met.police.uk/police-forces/metropolitan-police/areas/about-us/about-the-met/commercial-services/information-for-suppliers/become-a-met-supplier/).

## Information UCM Still Needs

1. **Maximum safe contract value:** turnover, cash, credit, working capital and the mobilisation UCM can finance.
2. **Operational capacity:** headcount, supervisors, relief staff, vehicles, hours, postcodes and simultaneous site starts.
3. **Pricing floor:** London Living Wage, NI, pension, holiday, sickness, TUPE, equipment, consumables, supervision, travel, reporting, inflation and minimum margin.
4. **Approved proof:** named references, case-study permissions, KPI history, complaints, rework, technical-cleaning evidence and photographs.
5. **Current compliance:** insurance scope and expiry, PPON, Cyber Essentials, ICO status, RAMS, COSHH, training and safeguarding.
6. **TUPE readiness:** adviser, due-diligence checklist, pension exposure, employee-liability information and mobilisation plan.
7. **Bid ownership:** one accountable owner, bid/no-bid score, clarification log, calendar, version control and final sign-off.
8. **Contract economics:** estimated annual value, gross margin, payment terms, mobilisation cash need, framework fee, win probability and downside case.
