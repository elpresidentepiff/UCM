# UCM Customer Hub Blueprint

## Product Goal

Give customers and commercial clients a simple way to request, understand, manage and verify UCM services without removing human support.

## User Journeys

- Homeowner or tenant: check service area, describe the property, upload approved media, receive a human-reviewed scope, manage booking and view completion evidence.
- Landlord or agent: request Handover Complete, track status, view evidence, resolve issues and repeat across properties.
- Office or facilities buyer: request a site review, compare care plans, access documents, monitor issues and review performance.
- Property manager: manage multiple sites, contacts, schedules, tickets, evidence, documents, reports and renewals.

## MVP Capabilities

- Service and postcode check.
- CleanScope enquiry form.
- Human-reviewed quote status.
- Secure customer account.
- Booking and recurring schedule view.
- Proof Passport records.
- Document center.
- Support and maintenance tickets.
- Review and rebooking prompts.

## Later Capabilities

- Multi-site account dashboards.
- Online approvals and payments.
- Building Health Reports.
- Care-plan optimisation recommendations.
- Partner and white-label access where justified.

## Trust and Privacy

- Show when AI assists a recommendation.
- Keep final scope and pricing human-reviewed.
- Collect only necessary data.
- Use explicit media and marketing permissions.
- Restrict site, customer and staff information by role.
- Provide phone and human support at every critical stage.

## Success Metrics

- Qualified form completion.
- Quote-response time and conversion.
- Customer self-service completion.
- Ticket resolution and satisfaction.
- Repeat booking, renewal and cross-sell.
