# UCM Internal Operations Center Blueprint

## Product Goal

Create one operational truth for leads, quotes, customers, sites, schedules, evidence, issues, contracts and marketing attribution.

## Core Records

- Organisations and contacts.
- Sites and access information.
- Enquiries and source attribution.
- Qualification and site visits.
- Quotes, revisions and approvals.
- Services, schedules and assigned teams.
- Contracts, values, renewals and margins.
- Proof Passport evidence.
- Tickets, complaints and resolutions.
- Campaign, content and partner sources.

## Pipeline

`Enquiry -> Qualified -> Site visit -> Quote -> Won/Lost -> Active -> Renewal`

Every stage records owner, date, next action and reason for movement. Lost work records a structured reason.

## Operating Views

- Enquiries requiring response.
- Quotes approaching follow-up.
- Today's sites and service risks.
- Open customer issues.
- Contracts approaching renewal.
- Capacity by team and zone.
- Revenue, margin and retention.
- Channel and campaign contribution.

## Controls

- Role-based access and audit history.
- No media sent to the Media Engine without consent status.
- No public claims generated from unverified data.
- Named owner for every lead, quote, ticket and renewal.

## Success Metrics

- Response and quote turnaround.
- Follow-up completion.
- Conversion by service and buyer.
- On-time service and issue resolution.
- Gross margin, utilisation, retention and expansion.
