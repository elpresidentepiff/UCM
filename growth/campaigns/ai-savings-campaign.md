# AI Savings Campaign

## Campaign Name

Smarter Cleaning Plans. More Economical Service.

## Core Thesis

UCM can use AI-assisted planning to reduce waste in the service process: faster quoting, better scheduling, clearer scopes, stronger route planning, smarter content and less admin drag.

This creates a credible "economical service" position without saying UCM is cheap or cutting standards.

## Approved Main Message

Because UCM uses AI-assisted planning, we can reduce admin waste and build more efficient cleaning plans. That helps us offer clearer, more economical quotes without cutting service standards.

## What We Can Say

- AI-assisted planning helps us quote faster.
- Smarter scheduling helps reduce wasted time.
- Clearer scopes help customers avoid paying for the wrong service.
- Efficient planning helps us offer economical cleaning support.
- Technology helps us spend less time on admin and more time on service quality.

## What We Must Not Say Without Evidence

- Guaranteed cheapest.
- Save 30%.
- AI makes cleaning cheaper.
- Lowest price in London.
- Same quality for half the cost.
- Fully automated cleaning service.

## FAQ

### Does AI clean the property?

No. UCM's trained team delivers the service. AI helps with planning, admin, scheduling, quoting and campaign intelligence.

### Does economical mean lower quality?

No. The goal is to reduce waste and improve planning, not cut standards.

### Can AI guarantee savings?

No. Savings depend on scope, location, frequency and property type. UCM can use smarter planning to build clearer, more efficient quotes.
