# Meta Campaign Readiness: Commercial Cleaning Quote Campaign

## Campaign Objective

Generate qualified quote enquiries for UCM commercial cleaning services in London.

## Campaign Status

Not ready to scale until tracking and landing page quality are confirmed.

## Audience

- London business owners.
- Office managers.
- Facilities managers.
- Property managers.
- Landlords and letting agents.
- Hospitality and retail operators.

## Offer

Tailored commercial cleaning quote for London businesses and managed properties.

## CTA

Request a commercial cleaning quote.

## Landing Page Requirement

Preferred page:

- Commercial Cleaning London landing page.

Minimum requirements:

- Clear service-specific headline.
- Phone CTA.
- Quote form.
- Proof block.
- Service scope.
- Location relevance.
- Tracking event.

## Creative Angles

1. Fewer supplier headaches: cleaning plus property-care support.
2. Better first impressions: clean offices, reception areas and communal spaces.
3. Property manager relief: cleaner communal areas, fewer complaints.
4. Sustainable cleaning: evidence-safe, professional process.
5. Commercial deep-clean reset: restore high-use spaces.

## Tracking Required

- Meta pixel.
- Lead/form submission event.
- Phone click event.
- Landing page view.
- Retargeting audience.

## Stop-Loss Rule

Do not scale spend if clicks are coming in but quote forms and calls are not tracked or are clearly low quality.

## Blockers

- Landing page quality not confirmed.
- Tracking not confirmed.
- Proof assets not collected.
- Budget not confirmed.
