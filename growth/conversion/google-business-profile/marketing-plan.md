# UCM Google Business Profile Plan

## Role

Google Business Profile is a high-intent conversion and trust channel for local service searches. Treat it as an operational asset, not a static listing.

## Actions

- Verify ownership and primary details.
- Correct category, services, service area, hours, phone and website.
- Add approved real photographs and videos regularly.
- Connect service-specific landing pages where supported.
- Record calls, website clicks and quote outcomes.
- Reply to genuine reviews professionally.

## Review Engine

After a successful completed job:

1. Confirm there is no unresolved complaint.
2. Send Google's official review link or QR code.
3. Do not offer discounts, gifts or incentives.
4. Record request date and response.
5. Reply without revealing private information.

Official guidance: https://support.google.com/business/answer/16816815?hl=en

## KPIs

- Search and Maps visibility.
- Calls and website visits.
- Genuine review request and completion rate.
- Review themes by service.
- Qualified leads and won work from Google.
