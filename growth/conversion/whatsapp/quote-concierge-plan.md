# UCM WhatsApp Quote Concierge Plan

## Role

Use WhatsApp as a structured conversation route for customers who prefer messaging and need to share context or approved property media quickly.

## Intake

- Name and contact permission.
- Postcode and service.
- Property or site type.
- Timing, urgency and frequency.
- Access constraints.
- Approved photographs or video where useful.

## Conversation Rules

- Explain that media supports a preliminary scope and may not replace a site visit.
- Never provide an AI-only final price.
- Offer a human handoff immediately.
- Do not request sensitive or unnecessary information.
- State response expectations.
- Record source, status and next action internally.

## Automation Boundary

Automation may acknowledge receipt, ask approved intake questions and create a lead record. A person reviews scope, exceptions, pricing and commitments.

## KPIs

- First-response time.
- Completed qualification rate.
- Human handoff time.
- Quote and contract conversion.
- Customer satisfaction and opt-out rate.
