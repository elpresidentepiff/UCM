# UCM Cleaning Services Reel V1

## Status: Rejected

Do not publish or use for paid promotion. This automated still-image and synthetic-voice concept was rejected on 2026-07-16 because it looks dated, lacks real UCM movement and weakens the company's authority. It remains only as a documented failed prototype.

Vertical campaign concept for Instagram Reels, Facebook, LinkedIn and YouTube Shorts.

## Creative Position

The film presents UCM as a professional London cleaning company built around people, method and visible proof. HOCl appears only as a controlled service in development because the exact product dossier and approved claims are not yet available.

Core line:

> Cleaned. Disinfected. Checked. Documented.

## Files

- `ucm-cleaning-services-reel-v1.mp4` - rendered 1080 x 1920 social video.
- `voiceover.txt` - approved evidence-safe narration draft.
- `subtitles.srt` - platform caption file.
- `render.py` - deterministic video renderer.
- `assets/` - generated concept imagery; these are not real UCM staff or client sites.

## Publication Gate

This is a rejected campaign concept, not final client evidence. Any replacement must:

1. Replace the generated slideshow with approved real UCM moving footage.
2. Replace desktop TTS with Carlos, a UCM team member or a directed professional human voice.
3. Confirm the exact HOCl product, UK status, label, SDS and permitted claims.
4. Confirm the quote URL and tracking parameters.
5. Obtain staff, site and client media permission.
6. Run the claims and consent review.

## Rebuild

The renderer requires Python, Pillow, `imageio-ffmpeg` and the generated WAV voiceover.

```powershell
python render.py
```
