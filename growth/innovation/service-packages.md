# UCM Service Packages

Prices must not be published until labour, travel, materials, risk, frequency and target margin are verified. These packages define value and scope, not final price.

## Essential Care Plan

For small offices, communal areas and straightforward recurring sites.

- Agreed scope and frequency.
- Named site contact.
- Standard checklist and quality checks.
- Clear issue route.
- Scheduled account review.

Commercial model: monthly recurring fee with exclusions and change control.

## Managed Care Plan

For property managers, larger offices and sites requiring cleaning plus responsive support.

Includes Essential, plus:

- Responsive-maintenance ticket intake.
- Monthly service report.
- Issue and resolution log.
- Approved service evidence.
- Cleaning and minor-works coordination.

Commercial model: higher recurring fee; maintenance follows an agreed rate schedule or separate scope.

## Intelligent Care Plan

For multi-site, high-service or reporting-led buyers.

Includes Managed, plus:

- UCM Proof Passport.
- Multi-site performance view.
- Quarterly Building Health Report.
- Human-reviewed AI-assisted planning.
- Trend reporting and quarterly optimisation.

Commercial model: premium recurring fee plus agreed projects.

## Handover Complete

For landlords, agents, developers and property managers.

- End-of-tenancy or new-build cleaning.
- Appliance, kitchen, bathroom, floor and detail checks.
- Minor repair and maintenance coordination.
- Snag observations without pretending to be a regulated survey.
- Approved evidence and completion summary.

Commercial model: fixed package for controlled property types; otherwise scoped project price.

## Building Health Report

- Cleaning quality status.
- Recurring defects and complaints.
- Minor-maintenance observations.
- Service evidence and unresolved items.
- Recommended actions with owner and urgency.
- Trend comparison where data exists.

Commercial model: included in Intelligent or sold as a quarterly review.

## Guardrails

- Define inclusions, exclusions and chargeable changes.
- Do not promise response times operations cannot meet.
- Do not present observations as regulated professional advice.
- Preserve human review for AI-assisted recommendations.
- Review margin and service quality before scaling.
