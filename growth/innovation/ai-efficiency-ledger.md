# UCM AI Efficiency Ledger

## Purpose

Turn AI-savings language into an evidence programme. Record where AI-assisted planning changes time, cost, quality or commercial outcomes.

## Baseline Metrics

- Enquiry-to-first-response time.
- Enquiry-to-human-reviewed-scope time.
- Administrative minutes per quote.
- Quote revision count.
- Travel time and distance.
- Scheduling changes and missed visits.
- Rework and complaints.
- Labour and materials variance.
- Quote-to-contract conversion.
- Gross margin by service.

## Experiment Record

Record experiment ID, owner, process, AI used, human decision point, baseline, comparison period, sample, results, limitations, customer impact, claim status and approver.

## Claim Levels

### Level 0: No Claim

The workflow is untested or data quality is insufficient.

### Level 1: Process Claim

> AI-assisted planning helps UCM structure enquiries and reduce avoidable administration.

### Level 2: Internal Quantified Result

Use internally when baseline, sample and methodology are documented.

### Level 3: Customer-Facing Quantified Claim

Requires commercial approval, a representative sample, clear methodology, limitations and review date.

## Review Cadence

- Weekly during experiments.
- Monthly for operating metrics.
- Quarterly for public claim eligibility.
- Immediately after material process or pricing changes.

## Prohibited Practice

- Selecting only favourable jobs.
- Treating correlation as causation.
- Converting time saving directly into customer saving without proof.
- Hiding quality deterioration.
- Publishing percentages without dates, sample and limitations.
