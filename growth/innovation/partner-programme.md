# UCM Partner Programme

## Purpose

Build tracked referral relationships with organisations that encounter cleaning, handover and property-care demand before UCM does.

## Priority Partners

- Letting and estate agents.
- Block and property managers.
- Developers and new-build operators.
- Inventory clerks.
- Concierges and resident teams.
- Office managers and flexible workspaces.
- Complementary maintenance specialists.

## Partner Offers

- Handover Complete referral route.
- Commercial or communal-site review.
- Emergency and short-notice qualification.
- Co-branded buyer guide.
- Partner-specific quote link and tracking code.

## Rules

- Commercial terms require written approval and appropriate disclosure.
- Never pay for fake reviews or endorsements.
- Do not share customer data without lawful purpose and permission.
- Confirm capacity before promising priority response.
- Track lead quality, revenue, margin and repeat work by partner.

## First 90 Days

1. Build a list of 30 organisations in the first five target zones.
2. Create one partner page and Handover Complete briefing.
3. Invite ten organisations to a manual pilot.
4. Measure meetings, referrals, quotes, wins and margin.
5. Retain partners producing appropriate, serviceable opportunities.
