# UCM Commercial CRM

Status: fictional, browser-only demonstration.

This folder proves the commercial workflow and interface direction. It does not authenticate users, persist data or connect to a production database. Do not enter real lead, customer, contract or compliance information.

## Tender and registration control

Open [`tenders.html`](tenders.html) for the operational buyer, portal and framework tracker. It includes:

- 105 individually tracked public-sector portals, London authorities, housing providers, property managers, developers, FM primes and accreditation routes.
- The current RM6264, Anchor Hanover, GLA, Kingston and TfL opportunity position.
- Editable registration, application, owner, deadline and contact history fields.
- The 12-item Level 1 starter pack requested from Carlos.
- GOV.UK registration, legal identity, finance, insurance, health and safety, operations, workforce, cyber security, environment, social value and commercial-bid requirements.
- Buyer-specific paperwork for councils, housing providers, FM primes, property managers, developers and specialist sites.
- An RM6264 readiness register covering identity, insurance, finance, policies and operational evidence.
- Local browser persistence and company-CRM CSV export.

The company CRM is preloaded with researched public information but does not claim that UCM has registered, contacted or applied where that has not been confirmed. Company progress is stored only in the current browser until the private production CRM is built. Sensitive source files must be kept in private, access-controlled storage and must never be uploaded to the public demonstration.

The advanced operating guide is [`growth/tenders/ucm-one-login-and-supplier-evidence-pack.md`](../../growth/tenders/ucm-one-login-and-supplier-evidence-pack.md).
