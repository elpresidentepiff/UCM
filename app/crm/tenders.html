Information gap

What UCM still needs before serious bids

Portals are discovery tools. These facts decide whether UCM can win, mobilise and make money.

01Maximum safe contract value

Turnover, cash, credit, working-capital requirement and the largest mobilisation UCM can fund without damaging the core business.

02Operational capacity

Current headcount, supervisors, relief cover, postcodes, working hours, vehicles and the number of sites UCM can start at once.

03Pricing floor

London Living Wage, NI, pension, holiday, sickness, TUPE, equipment, chemicals, supervision, travel, reporting, inflation and minimum gross margin.

04Approved proof

Named references, permitted case studies, KPI history, complaints and rework data, photographs and proof of technical-cleaning experience.

05Current compliance

Insurance limits and expiries, PPON, Cyber Essentials, ICO status, RAMS, COSHH, training, safeguarding and treatment-risk cover.

06Bid ownership

One accountable owner, bid/no-bid score, clarification log, submission calendar, document version control and mobilisation sign-off.

Level 1 starter pack

The 12 basics requested from Carlos

This is the proportionate starting point for smaller contracts and supplier applications. UCM will add contract-specific evidence only when an opportunity genuinely requires it.

01
Company identity

Exact legal name, company number, registered address and trading address.

02
VAT and UTR

Current VAT certificate or non-registration status, plus UTR confirmation stored privately.

03
Insurance certificates

Current Employers' Liability and Public Liability certificates, limits, covered work and expiry dates.

04
Health and safety policy

The existing signed policy and its latest review date. We review it before creating anything new.

05
COSHH and Safety Data Sheets

The products UCM actually uses, existing assessments and current supplier SDS files.

06
Accounts or turnover evidence

Latest accounts and current approximate turnover, supplied privately.

07
Staff and delivery capacity

Employee and supervisor numbers, regular subcontractors, service coverage and available capacity.

08
Services and prices

The services UCM can confidently deliver now, exclusions, hourly rates and minimum call-out.

09
Two client references

Two contactable clients who have approved UCM approaching them for a reference.

10
Two similar-work examples

Real scope, location, result and approved evidence that can become short case studies.

11
Training and relevant DBS evidence

Existing training certificates. DBS evidence is included only for roles that genuinely require it.

12
Existing operating documents

A current contract, cleaning checklist, inspection form or scope with sensitive client information removed.

Do not store the actual files here. Bank evidence, accounts, personal data and certificates belong in UCM's private document library. This public page is the list, not the filing cabinet.

Operating rule

One route, one owner, one next action.

Register selectively. Prioritise portals that expose London cleaning demand, not badges that produce no pipeline.

Bid selectively. Score margin, travel, mobilisation, payment terms, evidence burden and delivery capacity before committing.

Record honestly. A downloaded pack is not an application. A portal login is not an approved supplier appointment.

Protect margin. Capture levies, TUPE, wage requirements, consumables, supervision, reporting and mobilisation costs.
