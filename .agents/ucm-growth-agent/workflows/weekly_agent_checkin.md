# Weekly Agent Check-In

Use this every Monday.

## Weekly Status Format

### This Week's Commercial Focus

Name the service or buyer segment the week supports.

### Work Due

- Social posts due.
- Stories due.
- Reels/videos due.
- SEO tasks due.
- Paid campaign tasks due.
- Website tasks due.
- Reporting tasks due.

### Decisions Needed

List all decisions required from UCM or MS Infosys.

### Assets Needed

Examples:

- Before/after photos.
- Team photos.
- Client permission.
- Proof of accreditations.
- Insurance documents.
- Case-study details.
- Testimonials.
- Service pricing guidance.

### Risks

Call out:

- Missing tracking.
- Weak landing page.
- No proof for claim.
- Campaign without CTA.
- Slow approval.
- Creative bottleneck.

### Next 5 Actions

End with five concrete actions, each with an owner and due date.
