# Monthly Growth Sprint Workflow

Use this at the start of every month.

## 1. Review Last Month

Collect:

- Posts published.
- Stories published.
- Reels/videos published.
- Website changes shipped.
- SEO/GEO actions shipped.
- Paid campaigns launched.
- Leads, calls, forms, quote requests.
- Best-performing content.
- Worst-performing content.
- Client approvals delayed.
- Missing assets or proof.

If tracking is missing, mark the data as unavailable and recommend the tracking fix.

## 2. Choose Monthly Commercial Theme

Pick one primary theme:

- Office cleaning.
- Commercial cleaning.
- Property manager care.
- End-of-tenancy turnaround.
- Responsive maintenance.
- Data centre / IT cleaning.
- Sustainable cleaning.
- Seasonal deep cleaning.

Pick one secondary theme.

Choose one Wave 1 innovation experiment and define its owner, dependency, success metric and stop condition. Do not start a pilot without the required lawful data and human review.

## 3. Build Content Calendar

Create:

- 15 feed posts.
- 10 stories.
- 3 reel/video concepts minimum.
- 1 proof/case-study style post.
- 1 educational carousel.
- 1 direct offer post.
- 1 behind-the-scenes or team trust post.
- 1 sustainability post with evidence-safe wording.

For each core asset:

- Select Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn and Facebook adaptations.
- Assign the buyer, offer, CTA, landing page and UTM naming.
- Create the content manifest.
- Check the claims register and consent record.
- Assign Carlos-led content only where founder participation is confirmed.

## 4. SEO/GEO Actions

Each month choose at least five:

- Create or improve one service landing page.
- Add FAQ section to a service page.
- Write one SEO blog.
- Update metadata for a priority page.
- Add internal links.
- Create AI-search summary copy.
- Add or improve schema recommendation.
- Update Google Business Profile content.
- Build citation/listing consistency.
- Collect and publish proof assets.

## 5. Paid Marketing

Define:

- Objective.
- Audience.
- Offer.
- Creative angles.
- Landing page.
- Budget hypothesis.
- Conversion tracking.
- Retargeting pool.
- Stop-loss rule.

Do not scale spend unless tracking and landing-page fit are acceptable.

## 6. Website Conversion

Pick one conversion improvement:

- Stronger above-the-fold proof.
- Better quote CTA.
- Service-specific form.
- Trust block.
- Reviews.
- Case study module.
- Mobile hero fix.
- Sticky call button.
- Contact form improvement.
- Faster page speed.

## 7. Report

End the month with:

- What shipped.
- What moved.
- What did not move.
- Why it matters.
- What next month should prioritize.

Report channel performance separately. Connect content and paid activity to qualified leads, quote value, contracts, recurring revenue and gross margin wherever data allows. Update the AI Efficiency Ledger with verified admin, quote, travel, rework and margin observations; never infer a savings percentage from engagement data.
