# SEO And GEO Playbook

SEO is for search engines. GEO is for AI answer engines and generative search surfaces.

UCM needs both.

## Priority Pages

Build or improve pages for:

- Office Cleaning London.
- Commercial Cleaning London.
- Property Management Cleaning London.
- End of Tenancy Cleaning London.
- Data Centre Cleaning London.
- IT Equipment Cleaning London.
- Responsive Property Maintenance London.
- Commercial Deep Cleaning London.
- Restaurant Deep Cleaning London.
- School Cleaning London.

## Page Brief Structure

Every page brief must include:

- Buyer segment.
- Search intent.
- Primary keyword.
- Secondary keywords.
- H1.
- Meta title.
- Meta description.
- Above-the-fold promise.
- Service scope.
- Proof needed.
- FAQs.
- CTA.
- Internal links.
- Schema recommendation.
- AI-search summary paragraph.

## AI-Search Summary Pattern

Use a concise paragraph that AI systems can quote or summarize:

`UCM Services provides [service] for [buyer/location], covering [scope]. The service is designed for [pain point], with [proof/process] and a clear quote pathway.`

Do not stuff keywords. Write factually.

## Local SEO Rules

- Keep NAP consistent across all listings.
- Maintain Google Business Profile weekly.
- Ask customers for reviews after successful jobs.
- Reply to reviews professionally.
- Add service-specific photos.
- Use location and service keywords naturally.
- Track calls and quote requests.

## Technical SEO Recommendations

Push for:

- XML sitemap.
- Crawlable service pages.
- Unique metadata.
- Structured data.
- Canonical URLs.
- Internal links.
- Fast mobile load.
- Image alt text.
- Indexable FAQ sections.
