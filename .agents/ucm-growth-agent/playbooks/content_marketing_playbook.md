# Content Marketing Playbook

Content must support sales, search, trust, or retention.

## Blog Topics

High-priority blog ideas:

- How to choose a commercial cleaning company in London.
- Office cleaning checklist for London businesses.
- End-of-tenancy cleaning: what landlords and tenants should know.
- How property managers can reduce cleaning complaints.
- Why IT equipment cleaning needs specialist care.
- Data centre cleaning risks and best practices.
- Sustainable cleaning plans for offices.
- Cleaning and maintenance under one supplier: when it makes sense.
- Spring/summer/winter commercial cleaning checklist.

## Authority Content

Create assets UCM can reuse:

- Office cleaning checklist PDF.
- Property manager cleaning and maintenance checklist.
- End-of-tenancy handover checklist.
- Data centre cleaning risk checklist.
- Sustainable cleaning policy explainer.

## Case Study Format

- Client type.
- Problem.
- Scope.
- Constraints.
- UCM solution.
- Result.
- Proof assets.
- Next service opportunity.

Never invent client names or outcomes.
