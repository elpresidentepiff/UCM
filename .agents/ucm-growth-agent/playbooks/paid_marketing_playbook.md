# Paid Marketing Playbook

Paid marketing should be used carefully. UCM should not pay to send traffic into weak pages or unclear offers.

## Meta Campaign Structure

Recommended campaign groups:

1. Commercial cleaning quote campaign.
2. End-of-tenancy cleaning campaign.
3. Property manager / landlord campaign.
4. Retargeting campaign.

## Campaign Brief

Every campaign needs:

- Objective.
- Service offer.
- Audience.
- Geography.
- Landing page.
- Creative angle.
- Ad formats.
- Budget.
- Conversion event.
- Retargeting plan.
- Stop-loss rule.

## Stop-Loss Rules

Pause or revise if:

- No tracked leads after the test threshold.
- Cost per lead exceeds acceptable range.
- Clicks are high but forms/calls are low.
- Landing page mismatch is obvious.
- Creative attracts low-quality enquiries.

## Retargeting

Retarget:

- Website visitors.
- Form starters.
- Social engagers.
- Video viewers.
- People who clicked quote-related content.

Creative should use proof, not generic awareness.
