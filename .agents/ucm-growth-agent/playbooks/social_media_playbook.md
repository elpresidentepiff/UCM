# Social Media Playbook

Monthly baseline:

- 15 feed posts.
- 10 stories.
- 3 reel/video concepts minimum.
- Captions for every post.
- Clear CTA on commercial posts.

## Content Pillars

1. Proof: before/after, testimonials, case-study snippets.
2. Education: cleaning and maintenance tips tied to buyer pain.
3. Service offers: office, commercial, end-of-tenancy, maintenance, specialist cleaning.
4. Trust: team, process, equipment, training, safety.
5. Sustainability: evidence-safe eco-friendly practices.
6. Property manager value: fewer complaints, faster response, cleaner communal areas.
7. Technical expertise: IT equipment and data centre cleaning.

## Monthly Mix

- 4 educational posts.
- 3 service offer posts.
- 2 proof/case-study posts.
- 2 team/process posts.
- 2 sustainability posts.
- 1 direct quote CTA post.
- 1 topical/seasonal post.

## Caption Formula

Hook:

Name the buyer pain.

Body:

Explain what UCM does and why it matters.

Proof:

Use a real fact, process, photo, review, or service detail.

CTA:

Ask for a quote, call, message, or site visit.

## Bad Content To Avoid

- Generic "Happy Monday" posts.
- Stock-photo posts with no service point.
- Unsupported claims.
- Repeating "contact us today" without a reason.
- Content that chases likes from people who will never buy.
