# Social Media Playbook

Monthly baseline:

- 15 feed posts.
- 10 stories.
- 3 reel/video concepts minimum.
- Captions for every post.
- Clear CTA on commercial posts.

The fifteen core monthly assets are a source set, not fifteen identical cross-posts. Adapt the hook, format, depth and CTA for each platform.

## Platform Jobs

| Platform | Primary job | Core formats | Commercial CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Instagram | Visual proof and local discovery | Reels, carousels, stories | DM or guided quote |
| YouTube | Searchable authority and long-life education | Case studies, explainers, Shorts | Visit service page or request site visit |
| LinkedIn | Founder authority and B2B demand | Carlos posts, documents, native video | Book a commercial conversation |
| Facebook | Local trust, community reach and retargeting | Proof posts, short video, offers | WhatsApp, call or quote form |

Canonical ownership and 90-day execution plans live in `growth/channels/`.

## Content Pillars

1. Proof: before/after, testimonials, case-study snippets.
2. Education: cleaning and maintenance tips tied to buyer pain.
3. Service offers: office, commercial, end-of-tenancy, maintenance, specialist cleaning.
4. Trust: team, process, equipment, training, safety.
5. Sustainability: evidence-safe eco-friendly practices.
6. Property manager value: fewer complaints, faster response, cleaner communal areas.
7. Technical expertise: IT equipment and data centre cleaning.

## Monthly Mix

- 4 educational posts.
- 3 service offer posts.
- 2 proof/case-study posts.
- 2 team/process posts.
- 2 sustainability posts.
- 1 direct quote CTA post.
- 1 topical/seasonal post.

## Caption Formula

Hook:

Name the buyer pain.

Body:

Explain what UCM does and why it matters.

Proof:

Use a real fact, process, photo, review, or service detail.

CTA:

Ask for a quote, call, message, or site visit.

## Publishing Gates

Before an asset enters the Media Engine or publishing queue:

1. Create a content manifest using `growth/marketing/content-manifest.schema.json`.
2. Record service, buyer, location, campaign, hook, CTA and landing page.
3. Confirm customer, site, logo, testimonial and media consent where applicable.
4. Check wording against `growth/marketing/claims-register.json`.
5. Assign human approver and tracking parameters.
6. Record the published URL and connect enquiries to CRM source data.

No automated connection spam, invented reviews, unsupported guarantees or copy-paste cross-posting.

## Bad Content To Avoid

- Generic "Happy Monday" posts.
- Stock-photo posts with no service point.
- Unsupported claims.
- Repeating "contact us today" without a reason.
- Content that chases likes from people who will never buy.
