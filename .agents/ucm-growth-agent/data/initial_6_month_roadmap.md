# UCM Growth Agent Initial 6-Month Roadmap

Tentative period: March 2026 to September 2026.

This roadmap turns the quoted marketing services into a commercial execution plan.

## Month 1 - Foundation And Proof Capture

Primary goal: make UCM measurable and easier to trust.

Actions:

- Confirm tracking setup: GA4, Search Console, call tracking, form tracking, Meta pixel.
- Audit Google Business Profile and business listings.
- Collect proof assets: reviews, accreditations, before/after photos, team/process photos.
- Build first full monthly content calendar.
- Publish 15 posts and 10 stories.
- Create priority SEO/GEO page briefs.
- Identify top website conversion fixes.
- Prepare first paid campaign only if tracking and landing page are acceptable.

Success indicators:

- Tracking baseline exists.
- Proof asset backlog exists.
- First calendar shipped.
- Priority page list approved.

## Month 2 - Service Page And Social Authority Build

Primary goal: improve service-specific discovery and buyer confidence.

Actions:

- Improve or create pages for Commercial Cleaning London and Office Cleaning London.
- Add FAQs and AI-search summaries to priority pages.
- Publish authority content for office managers and property managers.
- Run Meta test campaign for commercial cleaning quote requests.
- Start retargeting audience build.
- Continue 15 posts and 10 stories.

Success indicators:

- Two priority service pages improved.
- Paid test has real conversion tracking.
- At least one proof-led post published.

## Month 3 - Property Manager And Maintenance Positioning

Primary goal: make UCM's cleaning-plus-maintenance advantage clearer.

Actions:

- Build Property Manager Care Plan messaging.
- Create Responsive Maintenance landing-page brief.
- Publish content around tenant complaints, communal areas, repairs, and supplier consolidation.
- Create a one-page sales PDF for property managers.
- Test retargeting creative using proof and process.

Success indicators:

- Property manager offer documented.
- Maintenance cross-sell path exists.
- Sales collateral exists.

## Month 4 - Specialist And Technical Cleaning Authority

Primary goal: separate UCM from generic cleaners.

Actions:

- Build Data Centre Cleaning / IT Equipment Cleaning SEO brief.
- Publish technical cleaning content with risk-control language.
- Gather evidence of process, training, products, equipment, or method statements.
- Create specialist cleaning social series.
- Test ads only if the landing page is strong enough.

Success indicators:

- Specialist cleaning page brief approved.
- Technical proof requirements identified.
- Specialist content live.

## Month 5 - Conversion And Retention Push

Primary goal: turn attention into recurring contracts.

Actions:

- Add stronger proof blocks and CTAs to priority pages.
- Build recurring retainer messaging.
- Create quote follow-up templates.
- Publish case-study format content.
- Review lead quality and optimize paid campaigns.
- Build customer review request flow.

Success indicators:

- Quote follow-up system exists.
- Retainer language is live.
- Review process is active.

## Month 6 - Scale Decision And Next-Phase Strategy

Primary goal: decide what to scale, stop, and systemize.

Actions:

- Produce 6-month performance review.
- Identify top channels by commercial value.
- Recommend next 90-day plan.
- Document best-performing creative angles.
- Document SEO/GEO improvements.
- Create next-phase budget and channel recommendation.

Success indicators:

- Clear keep/kill/scale recommendation.
- Next-phase roadmap approved.
- Reporting shows business impact, not just activity.
