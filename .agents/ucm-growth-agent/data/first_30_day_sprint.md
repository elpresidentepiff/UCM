# UCM Growth Agent First 30-Day Sprint

Purpose: get the agent operational and stop UCM from drifting into generic marketing.

## Sprint Outcomes

By the end of the first 30 days, UCM should have:

- A clean digital growth baseline.
- A proof asset backlog.
- A monthly content calendar.
- A priority SEO/GEO page list.
- A paid media readiness decision.
- A website conversion punch list.
- A reporting template ready to use.

## Week 1 - Baseline And Asset Request

Tasks:

- Confirm website access and analytics access.
- Confirm Google Business Profile access.
- Confirm Meta Business Suite / Ads Manager access.
- Request proof assets from UCM.
- Review current website pages and CTAs.
- Build keyword and page opportunity list.
- Confirm priority service for first campaign.

Deliverables:

- Access checklist.
- Proof asset request list.
- Priority page list.
- First weekly check-in.

## Week 2 - Content Calendar And SEO Briefs

Tasks:

- Build 15-post / 10-story calendar.
- Draft captions and creative notes.
- Create SEO brief for Office Cleaning London.
- Create SEO brief for Commercial Cleaning London.
- Draft AI-search summary copy for UCM's main service proposition.

Deliverables:

- Monthly content calendar.
- Two SEO/GEO page briefs.
- Caption batch 1.

## Week 3 - Creative And Campaign Readiness

Tasks:

- Prepare creative briefs for graphics and reels.
- Decide whether paid campaign is ready.
- If ready, draft Meta campaign brief.
- If not ready, list blockers and fixes.
- Create website conversion recommendation.

Deliverables:

- Creative brief pack.
- Paid campaign readiness note.
- Website conversion note.

## Week 4 - Publish, Measure, Report

Tasks:

- Confirm published content.
- Check engagement and lead signals.
- Review SEO actions shipped.
- Review website changes shipped.
- Build monthly report.
- Prepare next-month priorities.

Deliverables:

- First monthly report.
- Next-month action plan.
- Blocker list.

## Immediate Asset Request To UCM

Ask UCM for:

- Logo files.
- Brand colors and fonts, if available.
- Team photos.
- Before/after job photos.
- Reviews and testimonials.
- Accreditations/certifications.
- Insurance/compliance documents that can be referenced publicly.
- List of priority service areas/postcodes.
- Best-margin services.
- Services they want more of.
- Services they want less of.
- Existing customer objections.
- Any case-study-worthy jobs.
