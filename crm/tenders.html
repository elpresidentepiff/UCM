Operating rule

One route, one owner, one next action.

Register selectively. Prioritise portals that expose London cleaning demand, not badges that produce no pipeline.

Bid selectively. Score margin, travel, mobilisation, payment terms, evidence burden and delivery capacity before committing.

Record honestly. A downloaded pack is not an application. A portal login is not an approved supplier appointment.

Protect margin. Capture levies, TUPE, wage requirements, consumables, supervision, reporting and mobilisation costs.
