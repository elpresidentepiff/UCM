Paperwork UCM needs

This is the master pack. A buyer may ask for extra contract-specific information, but these are the documents UCM should prepare first.

01
Company identity and government registration

Certificate of incorporation, Companies House extract, registered address, VAT status, PPON, Find a Tender supplier record, connected-person details, exclusions declaration and current share code.

02
Accounts and financial capacity

Two to three years of accounts, current management accounts, cash-flow forecast, accountant contact, financial viability response, safe contract-value statement and secure company bank evidence.

03
Insurance certificates

Employers' liability, public liability, professional indemnity, business motor, products/treatment risk where relevant, policy limits, covered activities and expiry dates.

04
Health and safety

Signed policy, RAMS templates and examples, COSHH procedure, product register, safety data sheets, PPE matrix, incident/RIDDOR history and higher-risk task controls.

05
Cleaning operations and quality

Service catalogue, equipment register, quality inspections, complaints, corrective actions, business continuity, emergency response, waste controls, subcontractor controls and supply continuity.

06
Employees and safeguarding

Right-to-work process, employment and pay controls, training matrix, competency certificates, lawful vetting/DBS rules, safeguarding, equality, modern slavery and TUPE capability.

07
Data protection and cyber security

Data-protection policy, ICO status, privacy notices, retention schedule, breach response, access controls, MFA, backups, anti-bribery, conflicts policy and Cyber Essentials where required.

08
Environmental and social value

Environmental policy, measured carbon baseline, carbon-reduction plan, sustainable purchasing, waste evidence, local employment, training and contract-level social-value commitments.

09
Commercial proof

Capability statement, company profile, approved case studies, permitted client references, organisation chart, key CVs, geographic coverage, response times and service boundaries.

10
Tender response library

Method statements, mobilisation plan, staffing and resilience, TUPE assumptions, KPI report, governance, escalation, pricing model, risk register, bid/no-bid score and authorised declarations.

11
HOCl and specialist-service evidence

Product source, regulatory position, SDS, COSHH, staff training, verified concentration/contact time, efficacy evidence, surface compatibility, insurance and approved marketing claims.

12
Buyer-specific appendix

Council social value and FOI; housing safeguarding and resident care; FM supplier codes; developer handover evidence; technical-site security and shutdown procedures.

Do not store the actual files here. Bank evidence, accounts, personal data and certificates belong in UCM's private document library. This public page is the list, not the filing cabinet.

Operating rule

One route, one owner, one next action.

Register selectively. Prioritise portals that expose London cleaning demand, not badges that produce no pipeline.

Bid selectively. Score margin, travel, mobilisation, payment terms, evidence burden and delivery capacity before committing.

Record honestly. A downloaded pack is not an application. A portal login is not an approved supplier appointment.

Protect margin. Capture levies, TUPE, wage requirements, consumables, supervision, reporting and mobilisation costs.
